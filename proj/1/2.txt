Real-world operational system analysis: Supermarket
Variability

Customer arrival times vary randomly
    Different customers need different service times
    Number of items per customer varies
    Peak hours vs off-peak hours

Complexity
    Multiple checkout counters
    Different types of customers (few items vs many items)
    Staff schedules and breaks
    Interaction between queue length and customer behavior (leaving if too long)

Interconnectedness
    Checkout queues affect customer satisfaction
    Staffing decisions affect waiting times
    Waiting times affect customer arrival patterns
    Energy usage depends on number of open checkouts
